About Me

Hello, I'm Prudence Mnisi, born and bred in Pretoria, Mamelodi. I'm a poet and self-proclaimed pessimist, navigating the complexities of life
through the lens of my verses. I find beauty in the shadows often painting the world's uncertainties with my words. I believe that in the darkest moments
there's an opportunity to find profound thruths. words i live by: "Every adversity, every failure, every heartache carries with it the seed of an equal or greater benefit."
